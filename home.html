Pro shop

Proshop is where all the golf equipment are and this is also the place where visitors can book club hire.The proshop also gives customized T-shirts and cups with Golf club logo and even plain ones or visitors can customize it however they like.

Golf Courses

There are 3 different gold courses in the resort, The Sea view course has a sea view with 5 rounds of the 18 holes course along with club hire and proshop access included in the Five rounds package. The county course is in between the other 2 courses and can be included in the triple tee or Five Rounds and custom package, has 18 holes and has proshop access. The pinevalley course is the last course and is a pretty good one, has 18 holes and club hire to assist the player.
